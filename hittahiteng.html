How to get here

By car

From the E6, take the exit toward Gothenburg City Center and follow the signs to Heden. Turn right onto Sten Sturegatan and continue until you reach Teknikgatan. Parking is available in nearby parking garages, such as Heden Parking, which is just a 5-minute walk from the conference venue.

By bike

Gothenburg has well-developed bike lanes, and there are bike racks right outside the Better Web Conference. If you are cycling from the central station, take Drottningtorget and follow the bike lanes along Avenyn until you reach Heden. Then turn onto Teknikgatan.

On foot

The conference venue is within walking distance from Gothenburg Central Station. Follow the signs toward Kungsportsavenyn, and then walk for about 15 minutes via Heden until you reach Teknikgatan.

By public transport

Gothenburg's public transport is convenient and efficient. From the central station, you can take tram 5 or 10 toward Korsvägen. Get off at the Heden stop. Then walk for about 5 minutes until you reach Teknikgatan. You can also use Västtrafik's travel planner to find the fastest route.
