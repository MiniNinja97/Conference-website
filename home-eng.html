Welcome to the Better Web Conference 2024 in Gothenburg!

Are you a frontend developer or working in IT and tech? Then this is the conference for you! Over three inspiring days, from October 18 to October 20, frontend developers and tech enthusiasts from across the country will gather in the heart of Gothenburg to exchange ideas, share experiences, and get insights into the latest innovations in web development and modern technology.

The conference offers a wide range of seminars and workshops covering the latest trends in frontend development, including modern JavaScript frameworks, UX design, responsive web development, and performance optimization. In addition to exciting talks by industry-leading experts, you'll have the opportunity to participate in interactive workshops where you can apply new knowledge and technologies hands-on, right on site.

To enhance your experience, we are offering both meals and refreshments throughout all three days of the conference. Through our registration form on the website, you can easily select the days you wish to attend, specify your dietary preferences, and mention any allergies. Remember to register in time to secure your spot!

On the website, you'll also find our calendar, where you can view the full schedule of all seminars and workshops. This makes it easy for you to plan your days and select the lectures and sessions that interest you the most.
